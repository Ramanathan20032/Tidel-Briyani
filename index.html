About Us

Rooted in Tradition, Served with Love

At Tidel Biriyani, every plate tells a story of rich spices, authentic flavors, and timeless tradition. We craft the finest Biriyani with premium ingredients, aromatic basmati rice, and handpicked spices for an unforgettable taste.

From Dum Biriyani to Hyderabadi, Lucknowi, and Chettinad flavors, each dish is crafted with love. Dine in or take away, every bite brings warmth and authentic Biriyani essence.
